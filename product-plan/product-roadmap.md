This roadmap defines the sections (features) to be designed and built. Each section will have its own spec, sample data, and screen designs.

---

## Sections

### 1. accounts

**Role-Based Accounts & Permissions**

User authentication and authorization with three distinct roles: BCBA, RBT, and Caregiver. Manage user profiles, role assignments, and permission boundaries that control access to clients, programs, and data.

---

### 2. clients

**Client Profiles & Program Management**

Create and manage client profiles. View all programs assigned to a client. Auto-assign programs on save. Client-level overview with quick access to active programs and recent session data.

---

### 3. programs

**Program Creation & Stimulus Generation**

Build ABA skill-acquisition programs across four program types. Configure teaching vs. generalization modes. AI-powered stimulus generation for targets and images. Define teaching instructions and rerun policies.

---

### 4. review

**Human-in-the-Loop Review**

Review generated stimuli and images before use. Regenerate individual items that don't meet quality standards. Enlarge view for detailed inspection. Approve or reject generated content with feedback.

---

### 5. sessions

**Session Running & Data Capture**

Run programs with a consistent trial flow. Record correct/incorrect outcomes per trial. Apply rerun policies as configured. Generate session recaps. Works offline with sync when connected.

---

### 6. reporting

**Communication & Reporting**

Program-level comments with privacy separation by role. Session history and recap viewing. Data visualization with graphs showing trends over time. Export options for reports and clinical documentation.
